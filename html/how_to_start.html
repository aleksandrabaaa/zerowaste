HOW TO START


Beginnings can be tough but we prepared 9 simple ways to help you start your journey with zero-waste motion

SHOPPING

Remember to always bring your material or reusable bag with you to the shop. You can also take some containers, glass jars or paper bags. And try to buy package-free products.

PAPER TOWELS

Try to avoid using disposable, paper towels. Instead use material towels that you can wash and use for longer.

DISPOSABLE BOTTLES

Bring your own reusable or glass bottle with you to keep off plastic bottles. You can also accessorize with insulated cup so you don’t need to take a new cup every time when you’re in your favorite café.

BRIC-A-BRAC

Instead of throwing out your old clothes or furniture give them a new life. Maybe you can make a new pillow case from your old sweater? Or organize a garage sale for your neighbors.

PLAN IS ALWAYS A GOOD PLAN

Every time you go shopping make a list: what do you actually need. That way you’ll avoid buying products you’ve already have and save time and money. Maybe you don’t need staff that will one day end up in landfill?

SMART HOUSE

Use daylight as long as it’s possible instead turning on the light on 14 o’clock. Turn off all electrical appliances when you’re not using them or you’re going to sleep. When you go on holidays use ‘holiday mode’ in your fridge or freezer.

RECYCLE WELL

Recycle is good but it is not a solution of reducing you waste. Reduce the amount you recycle by reducing the amount you consume.

FORGET ABOUT FAST FOOD

Have you every been to one of the best-known fast-food restaurants? Then you know imagine how much packages are used. Plastic cup for cola, box for hot-wings and bags for big-mac with fires. Prepare your own lunch at home and bring it with you in your reusable container.

WE SAY NO TO PLASTIC STRAW AND CUTLERY

The same as with containers or bottles – avoid taking plastic straws, forks or spoons in take-away restaurants. Reusable ones don’t take a lot of space and can help the planet.
